Services

No matter your situation, Joseph can help you achieve your goals.

One-on-One In-Person

The most traditional form of personal training. Joseph will create a training program designed to meet each individual client's needs, and physical limitations, then walk you through every step along the way. Includes initial assessment, nutritional evaluation and program design.

$80 per hour

Small Group In-Person

Designed for families, friends, and co-workers to get fit together. Small groups consist of 3-6 people. Joseph designs a custom program for each person and supervises the training of the group to achieve their goals. Inlcudes individual initial assessment, nutritional evaluation and program design for each participant.

$100 - $200 per hour

Online One-on-One

Same level of service as One-on-One Personal Training delivered remotely via Zoom. Train where ever you are by simply logging on to your session and connecting with Joseph. Minimal to no equipment necessary. Includes initial assessment, nutritional evaluation and program design.

$65 per hour

Online Group Class

Joseph offers 60-minute online group classes delivered remotely via Zoom every Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 10am PST. Train where ever you are by simply logging on to class and following along. Minimal to no equipment necessary. Quick and easy set up to get started on your fitness journey now!

$20 per class

Mobile App New!

Want to work out on your own time but need a little guidance and coaching? Joseph now offers a comprehensive mobile app designed to deliver all the benefits of personal training efficiently and at a lower cost. Complete with initial assesment, nutritional evaluation, custom workout plans with demo videos, custom meal plans, goal tracking, meal logs and much more. Simply log on and get Joseph's expertise in the palm of your hand.

$50 per month

Custom Combination New!

Would you like to try the personal attention of One-on-One training but would also like to use the Mobile App? Are you a busy executive that travels throughout the month and would like a few Online One-on-One sessions? Or maybe you want to join in on the Online Group classes once in a while. Joseph can create a custom combination of each service for you. Each combination is designed to meet your special needs and schedule and can be adjusted as needed.

Price Varies

**All services are highly discounted when purchased as packages.
